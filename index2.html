How It Works

  1. Two Layers: Scrawl layer (shapes) and Fill layer (colors)
  2. Boundary Detection: Any non-white pixel in scrawl layer = boundary
  3. Flood Fill: Fills all contiguous white space until hitting boundaries
  4. Test It: Click Bucket, pick a color, click inside a shape
  5. Draw Custom: Use Draw mode to create your own shapes
